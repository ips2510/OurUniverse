A Very Serious Letter

Hi. It's me. I made you a whole universe.
With floating islands and butterflies and everything.
You're welcome. No, I'm not normal about you.

You are, objectively, my favourite person.
Even when you're annoying.
Especially a little bit when you're annoying.

I would absolutely pick you again.
In every timeline. Every universe.
Even the ones where we're probably raccoons.

Thank you for existing
and for tolerating me
and for laughing at the right moments.
